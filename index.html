Hi, I’m Shrek, and this is my page! I hope you like it. Here, you can learn all about me.

About me

I’m not your run-of-the-mill ogre. I’m a bit of a Marmite ogre: you either love me, or you’re scared to death at the mere idea that I live in a swamp half a mile from the village.

I hang around with an unusual crowd. There’s of course my lovely wife, Princess Fiona, Donkey, Puss in Boots (as seen on his own film!), Dragon, Pinocchio, the Gingerbread Man (aka Gingie) and many others.

For a spell, I was Duloc’s reigning champion pro-wrestler, though I prefer to settle my quarrels over a pint.

I like my privacy, though I’m always up for a karaoke dance party!

Favourite food

I’m something of a gourmet. Not only am I like an onion, but I also love onions! Here are some of my favourite foods:

  • Jelly squeezed from eyes (actually, it’s quite good on toast)
  • Stewed rodent (I make a mean weed-rat stew)
  • Spider’s web candyfloss
  • Lord Farquaad’s wedding cake

Favourite songs

You may be surprised, given my distaste for singing donkeys, that I am a bit of a music aficionado. My favourite songs, in descending order of preference, are:

  1. Just the Way You Are – Billy Joel
  2. Dance to the Music – Sly and the Family Stone
  3. I’m a Believer – The Monkees
  4. Like a Virgin – Madonna
  5. I’m On My Way – The Proclaimers
  6. Sandstorm – Darude
